Insulin resistance is a state in which a given

What causes high blood pressure?
High blood pressure usually develops over time.
It can happen because of unhealthy lifestyle choices, 
such as not getting enough regular physical activity.
Certain health conditions, such as diabetes and having obesity, 
can also increase the risk for developing high blood pressure. 
High blood pressure can also happen during pregnancy.

body mass index
Body mass index, or BMI,
is a way to help you figure out if you are at a healthy weight for your height.
Underweight: BMI is less than 18.5
Normal weight: BMI is 18.5 to 24.9
Overweight: BMI is 25 to 29.9
Obese: BMI is 30 or more
